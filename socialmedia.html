We can help you:

  • Create Facebook and Instagram ads, and/or optimize their outreach and effectiveness
  • Write engaging captions, descriptions, and headlines for posts

  • Develop a schedule for social media posts according to the audience of your business

Social Media is the fastest and easiest way to increase exposure, attract new customers, and share information with current customers. Facebook is one of the world’s most visited websites, below only Google and YouTube, and over 200 million Instagram users visit a business’s page daily. With each generation, social media only becomes more important. A Forbes study found that 78% of people said that social media posts of companies influence their buying decisions. That means more than the occasional post on Facebook; that means social media pages that exemplify the style and ideals of a business, that keep their viewers engaged.

Our Strategy:

Our process begins with looking through a business’s social media page and discussing what they like to share with their audience. Some of the questions we ask when beginning our work are:

  • What are you hoping to gain from your page? (i. e. new customers, a stronger relationship with the audience, an additional form of communication, a way to share relevant content)
  • Where have you noticed the most audience engagement on your page?
  • What methods have you employed to increase the exposure of your page to your customers and to the general public?
  • When do you usually post? How often do you post? Are you happy with the frequency of your posts?
  • Have you tried Facebook ads before? What results did you see? What targeted audience and time frame did you use?
  • Do you have ideas for your page’s content that you’re struggling to execute?
  • Is the majority of content on your page original or from other sources? Would you like more original content?
  • What do you hope to see from your business’s social media in the future? (i. e. expansion to additional platforms, more consistent posts, higher quality content)
