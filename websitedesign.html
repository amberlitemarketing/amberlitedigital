Website Design

A website provides consumers with a platform to view a business

Why a business website?

The first place modern consumers turn to when looking for a place to eat, to stay, or even to have fun is the internet. Therefore, creating functioning and enticing website design should be one of the first actions for every business. Even if your business isn’t looking for an average customer or wants a more personal interaction a website can be a great way to gain exposure for your business and filter for your business needs. A well-designed website can signify to consumers that you are a professional brand and provide a high quality service or product.

We can help with:

  • Choose an ideal domain and/or template based on cost and style
  • Design and format your website
  • Write concise, communicative content

Our Strategy:

To gain input for the format and style of website that would suit our client’s business, we start by asking a number of questions and allowing the client to choose his/her preferred template or a website to model off of.

We then work through the HTML and CSS to accommodate for client specifications and input all the text, images, and pages within the website. We either receive the content from the client and incorporate into the site or write text and choose images for the page based on client input. Whichever way the client prefers we strive to create the most professional website possible that suits the client’s needs, making sure to include all important keywords relating to the client’s business on the first page to improve on-site SEO.

Once the website is ready to be published we work with the client to identify the right domain name and extension. Finally, we help the client garner reviews and spread his/her website to improve off-site SEO.
