Tamil Nadu Foundation

Summary: A local nonprofit charity organization working to aid slow-learning children in southern India.

Goal: To promote their upcoming final event for the year through facebook, whatsapp, and email.

What we did:
→ Facebook/Email: Scripted and directed multiple videos led by the youth in the foundation to be posted in the weeks leading up to the event, along with a newsletter and formal invitation. → Whatsapp: Wrote short and captivating texts to be sent out in a mass message format by TNF leads to friends and family as both an invitation and to ask for donations.

Results: Prabhakar Santhanakrishnan, president of TNF Central Ohio Chapter, was surprised to see that his goal of $10,000 was exceeded by $5,000 and attributed a large portion of that success to our targeted campaign.
