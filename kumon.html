Kumon

Summary: An after-school tutoring center that helps students of all ages with math skills and reading comprehension.

Goal: Attract new customers, post more consistently on Facebook, create high-quality engaging content

What we did:
→ Facebook: Developed a consistent schedule where Kumon would post once a week at the peak day and time when their consumers would check Facebook.
→ Emails: Had emails created and sent out in tandem with Facebook posts giving consumers multiple platforms to receive accurate up-to-date information.
→ Handouts: Created innovative designs for invitations and handouts to be printed and given to Kumon families to communicate information directly.

Results: In the year we’ve worked with Kumon, the Facebook page’s follower count has more than quadrupled, and consumers now expect the weekly posts we make on Wednesdays. Mrs. Jindal, founder and CEO of Kumon Dublin-East has repeatedly expressed her gratitude for the service.
